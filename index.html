About Me

My name is Santiago Morris. I am a Senior in Electrical Engineering at North Carolina State University. I have thoroughly enjoyed my classes on circuit design, control systems, and programming. I love Electrical Engineering because there are so many different directions I can follow and I am constantly learning.

Projects

These are projects I have completed in class, at work, or on my own.This project was one I completed in a Logic Design class. It is a vending machine controller implemented with a mealy state machine. The machine has two selections A = 5 cents and B = 10 cents. It only accepts nickels and gives back the appropriate amount of change if necessary. The customer can only deposit up to 15 cents, it will return anything after that.

I completed this project during my internship with Energizer Holdings Inc. I programmed a new camera that replaced the old camera that was used to determine battery orientation. The machine tests battery voltage, and the camera is important because misoriented batteries cause jams. I programmed the Keyence camera using an imaging software that mapped circles on the battery. I then found the inputs and outputs used by the previous camera on the machines electrical schematics and replaced them for the new cameras I/O.
